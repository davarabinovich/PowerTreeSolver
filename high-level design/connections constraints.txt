
Forbidden connections:
	- Different types of loads to the same source
	- Constant current load to current source
	- Diode load to the voltage if their voltages differ
	- Diode loads of different forward voltages to the same current source
	- Diode loads to current sourcce if their total forward current doesn't equal to the source's current
	- Linear sources of both CV kindes to any current source
	- Different types of sinks to any current source
	- More than one converter to any current source
